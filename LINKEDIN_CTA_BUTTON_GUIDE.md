# LinkedIn Custom CTA Button Setup Guide

## What is the LinkedIn CTA Button?

A custom call-to-action (CTA) button appears on your company page header and makes it easy for visitors to take action. It shows up:
- On your company page header (desktop & mobile)
- On your company's posts in feeds
- In LinkedIn search results

## ğŸ“‹ Step-by-Step Setup

### Desktop Instructions:

1. **Go to Your Company Page**
   - Navigate to: https://www.linkedin.com/company/metabomaxpro/

2. **Click "Edit Page" Button**
   - Top right corner of your company page
   - Dropdown menu will appear

3. **Select "Add a Custom Button"**
   - Look for CTA button section
   - Or find "Custom Button" in left sidebar

4. **Choose Your Button Type**
   - Visit Website (recommended)
   - Learn More
   - Sign Up
   - Contact Us
   - Register
   - Apply

5. **Enter Your Destination URL**
   - URL: `https://metabomaxpro.com`
   - Make sure it includes `https://`
   - URL must be publicly accessible

6. **Add UTM Tracking (Optional but Recommended)**
   - Use this URL instead:
   ```
   https://metabomaxpro.com?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=company_page&utm_campaign=cta_button
   ```
   - This tracks visitors from LinkedIn in Google Analytics

7. **Save Changes**
   - Click "Save" or "Publish"
   - Button will appear immediately

## âš ï¸ Common Issues & Fixes

### Issue 1: "Button Not Appearing"
**Fix:**
- Wait 5-10 minutes for changes to propagate
- Clear browser cache
- Check in incognito/private window
- Verify you saved changes

### Issue 2: "Can't Find Edit Button"
**Fix:**
- Make sure you're an admin of the page
- Check Page Settings â†’ Admins
- Add yourself as Super Admin if needed

### Issue 3: "URL Not Working"
**Fix:**
- Ensure URL starts with `https://`
- Check for typos
- Test URL in browser first
- Make sure website is live and accessible

### Issue 4: "Button Not Showing on Posts"
**Fix:**
- Only shows on company page posts (not personal posts)
- Make sure you're posting AS the company page
- Refresh feed after setup
