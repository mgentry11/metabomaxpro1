Required Data Points

Basic Information (Required)

  • Name: Your full name or identifier
  • Age: Your age at time of testing
  • Gender: Male or Female
  • Weight: Body weight in pounds or kilograms
  • Height: Height in feet/inches or centimeters
  • Test Date: When the metabolic test was performed

Core Metabolic Metrics (Required)

  • VO2 Max: Maximum oxygen uptake (ml/kg/min)
  • Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR): Calories burned at rest (kcal/day)
  • Heart Rate Data: Resting, max, and target zone heart rates

Advanced Metrics (Optional but Recommended)

  • Fat Oxidation Rate: Grams of fat burned per minute
  • Carbohydrate Oxidation Rate: Grams of carbs burned per minute
  • Respiratory Exchange Ratio (RER): VCO2/VO2 ratio
  • Lactate Threshold: Point where lactate accumulates
  • Crossover Point: Where fat/carb oxidation shifts
  • Peak Fat Oxidation Zone: Heart rate range for optimal fat burning

How to Upload Your Data

Option 1: Upload PDF Report

If you have a PDF report from a testing facility:

  1. Log into your MetaboMax Pro dashboard
  2. Click "Upload Test Results"
  3. Select your PDF file (max 200MB)
  4. Our system automatically extracts the data
  5. Review and confirm the extracted values
  6. Generate your report

Option 2: Manual Data Entry

If you have results but no PDF, you can manually enter the data:

  1. Log into your dashboard
  2. Click "Enter Data Manually"
  3. Fill in the form with your test results
  4. Submit to generate your report

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my PDF doesn't extract correctly?

You can always use manual data entry. If extraction fails, our system will automatically prompt you to enter the data manually.

Can I upload multiple tests?

Yes! With our $39/month unlimited plan, you can track progress over time by uploading multiple tests and comparing results.

How accurate are the reports?

Our reports are as accurate as the data you provide. We use scientifically validated formulas and calculations used by exercise physiologists and sports scientists.

Do I need all the optional metrics?

No. We can generate a comprehensive report with just the basic metrics (VO2 Max, RMR, heart rate data). Advanced metrics provide more detailed insights but aren't required.
