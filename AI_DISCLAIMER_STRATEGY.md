## üí° KEY MESSAGES TO COMMUNICATE

### 1. AI Can Hallucinate
```
AI occasionally "hallucinates"‚Äîa technical term meaning it confidently states
things that aren't true. Think of it like that friend who's 80% right about
everything but the other 20% is completely made up with equal confidence.
```

### 2. AI Has a People-Pleasing Bias
```
AI is trained to be helpful and agreeable. If it thinks you want to hear
"yes, peptides will definitely work for you," it might lean that direction
even when the honest answer is "maybe, under these specific conditions."
```

### 3. VO2 Max Requires Specialized Knowledge
```
Not all healthcare providers are equally experienced with metabolic testing
and VO2 max optimization. Work with professionals who have specific experience
in this area‚Äîexercise physiologists, sports medicine doctors, performance
coaches who regularly work with metabolic data.
```
